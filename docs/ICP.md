# Ideal customer profile (ICP)

## Primary ICP — MVP (first 50–500 customers)

### Persona: “Launch-week founder”

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Role | Solo founder or technical co-founder |
| Company | Pre-seed to Seed B2B SaaS, 1–5 people |
| Event | Product Hunt, redesign, v2 launch, fundraise announce in **next 30 days** |
| Skill | No After Effects; may use Figma or screenshots only |
| Aesthetic taste | Cares about looking like Linear, Raycast, Vercel, Framer |
| Current solution | Static hero, Loom walkthrough, Canva template, or nothing |
| Budget | $19–49/mo feels cheap vs $500–2k freelancer |
| Pain | “I know our product is good but our launch looks amateur” |

### Jobs to be done

1. Ship a **30–45s launch video** for website + PH + LinkedIn this week  
2. Look **funded and designed** without hiring motion  
3. Avoid **AI spokesperson / VO ads** that feel cheap  
4. Reuse screenshots/UI they already have  

### Buying triggers

- Launch date locked  
- Competitor shipped polished video  
- Investor/advisor said “improve marketing presence”  
- Redesign went live without video  

### Disqualifiers (don’t chase early)

- Physical ecommerce (different templates)  
- Enterprise with 6-week brand/legal review  
- YouTube creators wanting vlogs  
- Needs perfect AE round-trip on day one

## First 50 paying users (concrete)

Target **indie devtool / B2B SaaS founders** in an active launch window.

### Where they are

- Building in public (Twitter/X)  
- Indie Hackers, Hacker News (Show HN upcoming)  
- Product Hunt upcoming page  
- DevTool Discords (Supabase, Vercel, etc. communities — careful, no spam)  
- r/SaaS (demo-led, not pitch posts)  
- YC Startup School network, local founder groups  

### How to reach (high touch early)

1. **Demo-first DM:** “I made a 30s launch video for [their product URL]—want the file?”  
2. **PH upcoming** commenters with weak video  
3. **Redesign posts** on Twitter—“congrats, did you ship video?”  
4. Waitlist → personal email with Loom of their URL rendered  

### Founding offer (first 14 days)

- **$29/mo** founding member (not free forever)  
- 10 videos/month, direct feedback channel (Slack/Discord)  
- Logo on site as early supporter (optional)  
- Goal: **5 paying users** = validation, not scale  

### Objections & responses

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| “I’ll hire on Fiverr” | Arco is faster and repeatable; export JSON if you still want a pro pass |
| “Canva is enough” | Canva is slideshow; Arco is kinetic UI + launch structure |
| “I need voiceover” | Top SaaS launches often don’t; text + music converts; VO later if demand |
| “Will it look AI?” | Show demo; music + real UI, no AI presenter |
| “One video then cancel” | Feature launch + ad sizes coming; second video in 30 days |
