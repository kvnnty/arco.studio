# Business

Category: **product marketing production** - motion-design automation for SaaS,
devtools, mobile apps, and product-owner websites.

Arco should become a professional SaaS that sells launch production outcomes,
not a generic AI video toy. The buyer pays because Arco saves days of founder,
PMM, or designer time and produces assets they can actually publish.

---

## Commercial Thesis

| Version | Business quality |
|---------|------------------|
| Generic AI video generator | Weak: novelty, low trust, high churn |
| Screenshot/recording enhancer | Strong MVP: clear buyer pain and visible value |
| Automated product-motion studio | Best: repeatable launches, teams, agencies, and campaign packs |

**Realistic solo target:** $5K-$25K MRR.
**PMF stretch:** $50K-$100K+ MRR.

**Who pays most:** PMM teams, founder-led SaaS, devtool companies, and agencies
with recurring product launches.

**What they buy:** a publishable launch asset package, not AI credits.

---

## Buyer Value

| Buyer pain | Arco value |
|------------|------------|
| Founder needs a launch video this week | Turn URL + screens + brief into a postable video in minutes |
| PMM needs repeated feature assets | Reuse brand, claims, pacing, and approved formats |
| Agency needs volume | Produce first drafts and variants faster while keeping real UI and brand |
| Product team lacks motion skill | Structured creative direction, motion presets, voice, music, and export QA |
| CapCut output feels amateur | Product-story beats, UI focus, quality checks, and professional handoff |

**Value anchors:** freelancer replacement $300-$2K per asset, 2-5 days saved,
faster launch cycles, social/hero/ad variants from one approved project.

## Monetization

Pricing should be tied to production value and professional deliverables.

| Tier | Price | Best for | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|----------|
| Intro | $19/mo | Founders validating a launch | 3 active projects, 1080p 16:9, Arco watermark optional only during beta |
| Pro | $49/mo | Indie SaaS and product owners | 15 active projects, 16:9 + 1:1 + 9:16, brand extraction, voice, music, custom music |
| Studio | $99/mo | Repeat launch teams | Unlimited active projects, 4K, campaign pack, subtitles, poster, transcript, version notes |
| Team | $199-$499/mo | PMM teams | Shared brand systems, seats, approvals, reusable campaign memory |
| Agency | Custom / volume | Studios and service providers | Volume projects, client workspaces, white-label handoff, priority render capacity |

Legacy launch pricing can be used tactically, but public positioning should not
train customers to see Arco as cheap AI minutes.

**Meter by:** active projects, delivery formats, campaign packs, seats, brand
systems, and render priority.

**Do not meter by:** failed renders, chat refinements, internal AI calls, or
normal revisions within an active project. Those are part of the production
workflow.

## Validation Path

### Phase 0 - Proof

Goal: prove that one narrow customer profile will publicly use the output.

- Create 10 manual or assisted videos for target SaaS/devtool products.
- Measure whether recipients would post the result unchanged or after one
  revision.
- Build the landing page around real Arco output, not generic promises.
- Capture objections about accuracy, quality, rights, and editing control.

### Phase 1 - First Revenue

Goal: 5 paying founding customers.

- Sell the outcome: "launch-ready product video package from your real screens."
- Run personalized outreach with a draft made from the prospect's product.
- Close manually, onboard manually, and watch every session.
- Charge enough to test value perception: preferably $49/mo Pro or a paid
  founding package, not free curiosity traffic.

### Phase 2 - Self-Serve Reliability

Goal: $2K-$5K MRR with a reliable golden path.

- Cold user can complete proof pack -> preview -> revision -> export in under
  10 minutes.
- At least 80% of beta exports succeed without developer help.
- First draft is good enough to revise, not discard.
- Product explains missing inputs and blocks weak claims.

### Phase 3 - PMF And Expansion

Goal: $20K+ MRR from repeat launches and campaign reuse.

- Add campaign packs and brand memory.
- Add team review only when single-player review is strong.
- Add agency workflows only when output consistency supports volume.
- Scale content and community around before/after launches, feature drops, and
  product marketing examples.

## Go-To-Market

**One line:** Turn your real product screens into launch-ready motion assets.

**Primary motion:** personalized demos. Send prospects a short video of their
own product, then invite them to refine and export it.

**Channels:**

- Founder-led SaaS and devtool launches.
- Product Hunt and launch communities after self-serve quality holds.
- Indie Hackers, X, LinkedIn, and product marketing communities.
- Agency partnerships only after repeatable handoff quality is proven.

**Launch checklist:**

- [ ] Real hero demo video under 30 seconds.
- [x] Polar checkout.
- [ ] Privacy, Terms, Cookies, and usage-rights copy.
- [ ] 3 beta testimonials with real output.
- [ ] Before/after examples against screenshots, CapCut, and Screen Studio.
- [ ] FAQ covering fake UI, voice, music rights, Figma, revisions, and export
  formats.

---

## Retention

Activation:

- User exports one 1080p MP4 they would publicly post.

Retention:

- User creates a second project within 60 days.
- User reuses the same brand system for a feature drop or campaign variant.
- User exports more than one format from an approved project.

Expansion:

- Campaign packs.
- Team review.
- Brand memory.
- 4K and social format bundles.
- Agency/client workspaces.

---

## Risks

- Output is merely "interesting" instead of publishable.
- Claims are unsupported or product screens are unreadable.
- Positioning drifts toward Runway, InVideo, or a generic video editor.
- Pricing anchors too low and attracts curiosity users instead of production
  buyers.
- One-and-done churn without campaign reuse.
- Render COGS rise before quality and payment are proven.
- Music, font, voice, or asset rights are unclear.

---

## Operating Rule

Every phase must answer: does this make Arco more likely to deliver a video a
serious product owner would publish or pay for?

If not, cut it.
