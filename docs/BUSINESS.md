## Monetization (current)

**Paid-only subscription** — project slots, not export credits.

| Tier | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| Intro | $9/mo | 5 active projects, 1080p 16:9 |
| Pro | $29/mo | 15 projects, all 1080p formats, brand, custom music |
| Studio | $59/mo | Unlimited projects, 4K, social export pack |

**Launch Offer:** $9 first billing cycle (coupon), then $29/mo Pro.

**Product rule:** Unlimited re-exports per project. Delete a project to free a slot when at cap.

**Metered:** Active projects only. **Not metered:** AI, draft, preview, chat, failed renders.

**Phase 2 teams:** $149–499/mo · **Phase 3 agencies:** $99–300/mo + volume

**Value anchors:** Freelancer replacement $300–$2K · Founder time 2–5 days → <15 min · Outcome: "launch this week"

**What kills pricing:** "AI video generator" positioning · inconsistent quality · one-and-done usage

## Market & competition

**Gap:** No product owns **SaaS understanding + opinionated motion + music-driven export** without AI fake UI or generic VO slideshows.

**Real competition:** Freelancers, agencies, founder stack (screenshots → CapCut → Canva)

| Layer | Leaders | Gap |
|-------|---------|-----|
| AI video clips | Runway, Veo | No SaaS story; weak UI control |
| Marketing video SaaS | InVideo, Pictory | Generic; VO feel |
| Motion precision | AE, Rive | Manual |
| Easy templates | Canva | Not agency taste |
| Code render | Remotion | Arco builds on top |

**Moat to build:** Template taste · SaaS story structure · real UI pipeline · retention loops · scene JSON escape hatch

**Do not compete on:** Cheapest AI minutes · general editing · B2C creator features

## Go-to-market

**One line:** The fastest way for SaaS to turn a product URL into a launch-ready marketing video—music, motion, and your real UI.

### Phase 0 (now)

Waitlist live · killer demo MP4 · 10 manual "gift" videos for dream ICPs

### Phase 1 — First revenue (14 days)

**Goal:** 5 × $29 founding = $145 MRR validation

Ship demo → landing + Polar → 20 personalized DMs with **their** product video → onboard 5 paid manually

### Phase 2 — PMF (months 1–6)

**Goal:** $2K → $5K MRR — Twitter before/after, Product Hunt when self-serve works, Indie Hackers, SEO, Remotion community

### Activation / retention

- **Activation:** User exports one 1080p MP4 they'd post publicly
- **Retention:** Second project within 60 days

### Launch checklist (self-serve)

- [ ] Demo video on hero (<30s)
- [x] Polar checkout
- [ ] Privacy + Terms
- [ ] 3 beta testimonials
- [ ] FAQ: AE? VO? Figma?
