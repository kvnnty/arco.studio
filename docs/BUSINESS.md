## Models

### A — Self-serve (now)
Founders, indies, hackathons · $15–79/mo

### B — Teams (PMF)
PMM, growth · $149–499/mo

### C — Agencies (later)
Studios · $99–300/mo + volume

## Money engines

1. **Recording enhancement** — core job (MVP)
2. **Feature ship loop** — export per release (retention)
3. **Ad variants** — 9:16, hooks (expansion)
4. **Agency volume** — ARPU multiplier

## What people pay for

- Replaces $300–5k freelancer / CapCut hours
- Raw recording → polished demo (before/after)
- Ship deadline (hackathon, launch)

## Risks

- Quality < 95%
- One-and-done churn
- CapCut "good enough"
- Render COGS
