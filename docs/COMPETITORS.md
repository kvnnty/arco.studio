# Competitive landscape

Full map of adjacent products and Arco’s gap. Arco does **not** compete on raw AI video generation.

## The gap Arco fills

No dominant product combines:

1. **SaaS understanding** (URL → story)  
2. **Opinionated motion design system** (After Effects–level taste, templated)  
3. **Orchestration** (script structure + automation + music + real UI)  

| Layer | Who does it well | Gap |
|-------|------------------|-----|
| AI video clips | Runway, Veo, Luma, Kling, Pika | No SaaS story; weak UI control |
| Marketing video SaaS | InVideo, Pictory, Fliki, Creatify | Generic; VO/slideshow feel |
| Motion precision | After Effects, Rive | Manual; high skill |
| UI motion (manual) | Jitter | Not automated launch pipeline |
| Easy templates | Canva | Not agency taste |
| Code render | Remotion | No product layer |
| UGC ads | Arcads | AI actors; different category |

**Real competition:** freelancers, agencies, founder 5-tool stack (screenshots → CapCut → Canva).

| Product | Strength | Weakness vs Arco |
|---------|----------|------------------|

| **Runway** | Motion control, ads | Not SaaS-native; not motion system |
| **Luma Dream Machine** | Realistic camera | No marketing intelligence |
| **Kling** | Product physics | Clip factory, not launch narrative |
| **Pika** | Fast social clips | Not professional SaaS promo |
| **Creatify** | URL → ecommerce ads | Not devtool aesthetic |

**Arco stance:** Optional B-roll in Phase 2 only—never hallucinate UI.

## Positioning vs each (one line)

| Competitor | Arco difference |
|------------|-----------------|
| Runway | We ship **launch narratives** with your UI, not clips |
| Canva | **Crafted motion**, not templates |
| InVideo | **No AI voiceover**; music + text like top SaaS launches |

| Agency | **10 minutes vs 2 weeks**, 10× cheaper |
| Remotion | We're the **product**; they're the engine |

---

## Competitive moat (what to build)

1. **Template taste** — opinionated motion system  
2. **SaaS story structure** — scene types that convert  
3. **Real UI pipeline** — screenshots/Figma, not generated UI  
4. **Retention loops** — feature videos, ad variants  
5. **scene JSON** — designer escape hatch without parsing `.aep`  

---

## What not to compete on

- Cheapest AI minutes  
- Most video models  
- General-purpose editing  
- B2C creator features
