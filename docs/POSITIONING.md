# Positioning & messaging

## Category name

**SaaS launch motion system** / **SaaS launch video engine**

Avoid: “AI video generator”, “text to video”, “AI voiceover”, “Create videos with AI”.

## One-liner options

- Turn your product URL into a music-driven launch video—with motion graphics and your real UI, no After Effects.
- Launch videos for SaaS, styled like Linear and Raycast: motion, music, product UI—no narrator required.
- From landing page to launch video in minutes. Designed, not generated.

## Headline / sub (landing, post-waitlist)

- **Headline:** SaaS launch videos that look designed, not generated.
- **Sub:** Paste your URL, add screenshots, pick a track—export a polished promo in minutes. No voiceover required.
- **Proof:** Embedded demo video (killer demo is the product).

## How it works (3 steps)

1. **Import** — URL, screenshots, or Figma frames
2. **Storyboard** — Arco writes on-screen text scenes; you edit headlines
3. **Export** — Music + motion template → MP4 (+ scene JSON for pros)

## Reference brands (creative north star)

Linear, Raycast, Vercel, Framer—music, UI, motion, text callouts. Not infomercial VO.

## Objection handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| “I need a voiceover” | Many top SaaS launches don’t use one; text + music converts. VO can come later. |
| “Runway does video” | Runway makes clips; Arco makes **launch narratives** with your UI. |
| “Canva is enough” | Canva is template-slideshow; Arco is **kinetic UI + launch structure**. |
| “I need After Effects” | Arco for v1; export JSON; AE plugin later for polish. |
