# Market & competition

## Market context

Arco sits between:

- AI video generators (large, crowded, price-sensitive)
- Design tools (Canva, Figma, AE)
- Marketing video SaaS (InVideo, Pictory)

Demand drivers:

- Constant SaaS launches and feature releases
- Short-form video for ads and social
- Founders avoiding $300–$2k motion designers
- Trend toward **music + UI + text** launch films (not talking-head ads)
- Weak “good enough” tools → willingness to pay for **conversion-quality** output

## Adjacent tools (not full Arco)

### AI text/image-to-video

- **Google Veo** — cinematic clips, not structured SaaS promos
- **Runway** — strong motion; weak marketing structure / UI control
- **Kling, Pika, Luma Dream Machine** — clips; no launch story system
- **Creatify** — URL → ecommerce ads; different motion bar

### Marketing video SaaS

- **InVideo AI, Pictory, Fliki** — stock + automation + often VO; generic feel

### Motion / design

- **Canva** — templates, not agency taste
- **Jitter** — clean UI motion; manual
- **Rive** — interactive UI; not marketing video generator
- **Remotion** — render engine; build Arco on top

### UGC / presenter ads

- **Arcads** — AI actors; different category from Arco MVP

## Honest gap (opportunity)

No product owns end-to-end:

**SaaS understanding (URL → story) + opinionated motion system + music-driven export**

without forcing AI voiceovers or stock slideshows.

## Competition that matters most

- Freelance motion designers
- Agencies
- Founder toolchain: screenshots → CapCut → (optional bad VO) → Canva

## Financial thesis

See [MONETIZATION.md](./MONETIZATION.md).

- Commodity “AI video tool”: $10–30/mo, high churn
- Niche “SaaS launch motion engine”: $30–150/mo, better retention
- Money follows: **conversion impact**, **time replacement**, **speed to launch**

## Where Arco does NOT fit

| ❌ Category | Why |
|------------|-----|
| Pure B2C creators | Low WTP, CapCut, no ROI story |
| General video editor | Canva, CapCut — no moat |
| AI video generator | Commoditized pricing |
| UGC / AI presenter | Arcads category |
| Enterprise (early) | Long sales, brand/legal |

See [BUSINESS.md](./BUSINESS.md).

## Risk

- Shallow product → easy to copy, race to bottom
- Output quality below freelancer → no retention
- Positioning as Runway or VO-ad clone → death by comparison
- Adding VO too early → “generic AI ad” perception
- One-and-done launch-only usage → churn
