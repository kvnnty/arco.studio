## FAQ

**Do I need After Effects?**  
No. Arco renders for you. Pros can export scene JSON for AE later.

**Does it use AI voiceovers?**  
No. Arco uses music + on-screen text—like many Linear and Raycast launches. Clearer and more premium.

**Can I use my real product UI?**  
Yes. Upload screenshots or Figma exports. We don’t hallucinate your dashboard.

**How long does it take?**  
Most founders go from URL to MP4 in under 15 minutes.

**Is this another AI video generator?**  
No. Arco is a **SaaS launch motion system**—structured story, your UI, designed motion.

**What about Figma?**  
Export frames as PNG for MVP; Figma plugin coming later.

**Can agencies use it?**
