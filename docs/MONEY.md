## Path to 10/10

1. **Arco Project** = system of record (not export-only)
2. **Every ship** → new recording → re-export (GitHub/Linear integrations)
3. **Best click-motion** on real recordings (beat Screen Studio + CapCut)
4. **One project → many formats** (16:9, 9:16, GIF, hooks)
5. **Viral free tier** → B2B team expansion
6. **Enterprise + marketplace** (year 3+)

## Risks

- Output < 95% quality → no pay
- Competing with CapCut on price
- One-and-done usage
- Screen Studio adds marketing workflow
- Render COGS without metering

## Bottom line

> Motion-design productivity for product demos, with AI automating repetitive parts — stronger than "another AI video generator."
