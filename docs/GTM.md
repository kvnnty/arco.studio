# Go-to-market

## Positioning (one line for all channels)

> The fastest way for SaaS to turn a product URL into a launch-ready marketing video—music, motion, and your real UI. No agency. No After Effects.

---

## Phase 0 — Pre-product (now)

| Action | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| Waitlist live | Capture intent (`WAITLIST_WEBHOOK_URL`) |
| Killer demo MP4 | One URL → 30s video (manual or scripted pipeline) |
| 10 manual “gift” videos | Outreach ammunition for dream ICPs |

---

## Phase 1 — First revenue (14 days)

**Goal:** 5 founding members × $29 = **$145 MRR** (validation, not scale).

| Day | Task |
|-----|------|
| 1–3 | Ship one Remotion template + demo render from real SaaS URL |
| 4 | Landing: embedded demo + Stripe $29 founding link |
| 5–6 | List 30 founders with PH upcoming / recent launch tweets |
| 7–10 | 20 personalized DMs with **their** product video attached |
| 11–12 | 5 waitlist emails with custom preview |
| 13–14 | Onboard 5 paid users manually (you run pipeline, they send URL + screenshots) |

### Metrics (14-day)

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Demos sent | 20 |
| Replies | 5+ |
| Paid founding | 5 |
| Time to deliver | &lt;24h per manual customer |

### What not to do in 14 days

- Product Hunt launch (need stable self-serve)  
- Paid ads  
- Free unlimited tier  
- VO / avatar features  

---

## Phase 2 — PMF signal (months 1–6)

**Goal:** $2K → $5K MRR.

| Channel | Tactic |
|---------|--------|
| Twitter/X | Before/after clips, build in public, “made this in 10 min” |
| Product Hunt | Launch when self-serve URL→MP4 works |
| Indie Hackers | Story post + demo |
| SEO (long) | “SaaS launch video”, “product demo video tool” |
| Partnerships | Remotion community, startup newsletters |
| Referral | 1 free month for referral (after billing exists) |

### Content pillars

1. **Side-by-side:** Canva template vs Arco (same screenshots)  
2. **URL → video timelapse** (screen recording)  
3. **Deconstruct** Linear/Raycast-style launches (educational, not rip-off)  

---

## Phase 3 — Scale niche (months 6–18)

**Goal:** $20K+ MRR, introduce Model B.

- Case studies with MRR/conversion quotes (even qualitative)  
- Team seats + brand kit  
- 9:16 ad variants  
- Outbound to Seed SaaS PMMs (light, demo-led)  
- Agency tier waitlist  

---

## First 100 customers (funnel)

```
Waitlist / Twitter / PH
        ↓
   Killer demo (5 sec hook)
        ↓
   Founding $29 or Starter $19
        ↓
   Activation: first MP4 in <15 min
        ↓
   Retention: second video (feature launch / ad)
```

### Activation definition

User exports **one 1080p MP4** they would post publicly.

### Retention definition

User returns within **60 days** for second project.

## Launch checklist (self-serve ready)

- [ ] Demo video on hero (&lt;30s, muted + music)  
- [ ] Live “Try with your URL” or waitlist if not ready  
- [ ] Stripe checkout  
- [ ] Privacy + Terms links (not `#`)  
- [ ] 3 testimonial quotes (beta users)  
- [ ] FAQ: AE? VO? Figma? (see [LANDING.md](./LANDING.md))
